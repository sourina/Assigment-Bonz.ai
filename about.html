A Classy Resort in Darjeeling

Centrally located near the 'Mall' and 'Chowrasta', our resort is an ultimate source of peace and tranquillity. Don't believe? Come here and we bet you will have no other option but to believe. As the classiest resort in Darjeeling, the resorts' staff is always there to greet you with warmth. Born with the exquisiteness and treasures of antiquity, with the trails of English era charm, evident in each slice of the building, Central Heritage Resort & Spa, Darjeeling, 2 minutes walking distance from Mall Road, stands proud as one of the acknowledged landmarks of Darjeeling and has remained talk-of-the-town for rendering lavish 'British Raj' experience to its guests since its inception in 1905.
Central Heritage Resort & Spa features tastefully crafted and spaciously appointed rooms laid with wooden flooring, and modern amenities and also possess the largest banqueting space in Darjeeling. The resort composes a serene ambience in the evening with a pianist playing the piano around an aquarium and cascade.

Nestled in the heart of Darjeeling city, our Banquet Halls offers an impressive portfolio of spacious and well-decorated event space perfectly apt for grand wedding ceremonies as well as wedding reception parties. We can conveniently accommodate 200 guests and provide them with lavish hospitality that corresponds well with Darjeeling's heritage and culture. Our professional concierge team will guide you from the inception to execution of your most memorable days. We will also satisfy your taste buds with authentic flavours and mouth-watering cuisine curated by our in-house culinary experts.
